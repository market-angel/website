Story

I believe I began making books in first grade. I may share these in the future, if I can find them. Honestly, I don't think they were that bad considering how old I was. I had someone who would help me write them, but I can't remember who they were. We would distribute the books for kids to read in class, and I remember them being quite hilarious. Eventually I moved to 2nd grade, which was a new school and all my friends were gone once again. If I even had any, I can't remember. It was around this time when Derpy Studios was born (not the YouTube channel, the corporation) Yes, I made kids at my school the "employees" and made them give me ideas. And then this basically carried on until now, where I'm trying to piece together my own timeline, which has gone way further than I anticipated.
